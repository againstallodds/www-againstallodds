Kodak and Cyberspace



ODAK has supported Rick Smolan's projects for the past 15 years. Our support of the "24 Hours In Cyberspace" project differs from previous projects because this time Kodak is bringing much more to the table in terms of product and technology support. In addition to supplying the color negative film for the project, we are also supplying technical support via our Digital Science products. The "24 Hours in Cyberspace" project would not be possible without Kodak's expertise in both traditional and digital photography.
