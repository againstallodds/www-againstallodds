Andy Levin

UNITED STATES

Andy Levin is a contributing photographer at Life and a regular contributor to People. A participant in ten Day in the Life projects including America, Levin has won awards from the Art Directors' Club, Communication Arts Magazine and the National Press Photographers Association's Pictures of the Year. His whimsical look at Coney Island was recently the cover story of Reportage and is part of an ongoing self-assigned project documenting beach culture all over the world. He has been a professional photojournalist for twenty years.
