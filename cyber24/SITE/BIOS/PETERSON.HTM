David Peterson

UNITED STATES

David Peterson has won two Pulitzer Prizes: one for feature photography in 1987, and another for Public Service in 1991. He was a Nikon/NPPA Sabbatical Recipient in 1986, and won the National Press Photographers Region Five Photographer of the Year from 1978 to 1980.
