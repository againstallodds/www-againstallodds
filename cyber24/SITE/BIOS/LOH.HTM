David Loh

MALAYSIA

David Loh has worked as a professional photographer for the past five years. For the past year he has been with REUTERS; before that he spent a year as picture editor for a business weekly, The Edge. From 1991 to 1993 he worked for a national paper, The Star. He had his first solo exhibition in 1993, entitled "Fragments of an Arrival."
