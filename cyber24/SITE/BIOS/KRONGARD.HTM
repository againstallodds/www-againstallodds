Steve Krongard

UNITED STATES

Steve Krongard is an award winning photographer who lives with his wife and son in a 1790 farmhouse outside of New York City. He shoots in his SoHo studio, as well as around the world for a variety of blue chip clients in diverse industries, including airlines, technology, banking, tourism, manufacturing and advertising. The nature of his photography is an appropriate response to the assignment at hand, often translated into strong graphics and rich colors. The work ranges from landscapes and large productions on location, to reportage and journalism, as well as special effects and still lifes in the studio. Because of this versatility, his pictures have been seen in most major magazines, as well as numerous annual reports and advertising campaigns.
