Arnaud De Wildenberg

FRANCE

Arnaud de Wildenberg is a lawyer who got his start in photography in 1979. He has covered news stories for Time, Newsweek, STERN, and Paris Match. His work has taken him to Poland, Cambodia, Israel, Iran, Iraq, Lebanon, and elsewhere. He has received awards from Paris Match (1987), the World Press Prize for Poland in 1982, and the World Press Science-Technology Prize in 1990. He currently concentrates on freelance feature stories and corporate work, and is affiliated with the Gamma and the Sygma agencies.
