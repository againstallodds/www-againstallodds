Adrian Bradshaw

CHINA

Adrian Bradshaw's academic background in China and economics led him to China in 1984. He has been based in China for more than a decade. During that time, he has worked on assignment for most major news magazines, such as Newsweek and Der Spiegel, as well as a variety of corporate clients from BP to AT&T.
