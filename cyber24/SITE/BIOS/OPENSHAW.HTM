Sean Openshaw

UNITED STATES

Sean Openshaw is chief photographer at the Arizona Daily Sun in Flagstaff, Arizona. He is a graduate of Arizona State University. He interned for the Arizona Republic and free-lanced for the Mesa Tribune before being hired at the Sun three years ago. Sean teaches basic photojournalism at Northern Arizona University. He also has a freelance business that ranges from photographing weddings to covering news on the reservation and Grand Canyon for papers around the country. Awards include AP Photo Awards in feature, news and sports.
