Wesley Bocxe

BOSNIA

Wesley Bocxe most recently has been working for JB Pictures based in Mexico City. His work has taken him to Somalia, Rwanda, Haiti, and Bosnia-Herzegovina. He has also been employed by Reuters and SIPA in the past ten years. He has covered news stories throughout Central America for magazines such as Time, Newsweek, U.S. News & World Report, and The New York Times. While at SIPA, he was based in El Salvador covering stories in Central and South America as well as the Gulf War. He has been awarded Picture of the Year in both 1989 and 1991.
