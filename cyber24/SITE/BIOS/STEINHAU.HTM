Peter Steinhauer

UNITED STATES

Peter Steinhauer has been living in Vietnam since December of 1993 working on a black and white book project, Vietnam: Portraits and Landscapes. He is now finished, and plans to move back to Hanoi and open up a studio where he will photograph advertising work to pay the bills. He will continue to photograph the Vietnam that is still untouched by modern, Western society but is quickly disappearing.
