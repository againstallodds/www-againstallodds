R. Ian Lloyd

SINGAPORE

Ian Lloyd was born in Canada and has been based in Singapore since 1980. He formed his own company, R. Ian Lloyd Productions, in 1983 and has since photographed more than 28 books on Asia. His library provides stock photos from his own Asian collection as well as images from agencies around the world. His award-winning photography has appeared on the covers of Newsweek, Fortune, Travel Holiday, National Geographic Traveler, Gourmet and BusinessWeek magazines.
