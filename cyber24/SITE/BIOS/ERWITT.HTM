Misha Erwitt

UNITED STATES

Misha Erwitt is a native New Yorker who began taking pictures at age 11. His work has been published in Time, Newsweek, Esquire, and the New York Times magazine. He has participated in eight Day in the Life projects. He is presently on the staff of the New York Daily News.
