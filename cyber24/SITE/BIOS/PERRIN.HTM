Ward Perrin

CANADA

Ward Perrin graduated top of his class from the Western Pacific Academy of Photography in 1987, and received the Freeman Patterson Award for best portfolio. He has worked as a news photographer and photo editor for The Edmonton Journal, the Brandon Sun, and the Vancouver Sun dailies, and more recently for The Canadian Press, where he covered the Clinton/Yeltsin Summit, the World Cup, the NHL playoffs and numerous Canadian political events. Currently he works as a picture editor and photographer for the Vancouver Sun.
