Ricardo Azoury

BRAZIL

Ricardo Azoury was born in Brazil, and has been a photojournalist since 1977. He is especially interested in social and cultural documentation, and has been published in UESA, Manchete(Brazil), Time, Scientific American, Audubon, and the Sunday Telegraph. At the moment, he is producing several CD-ROMs on Brazil.
