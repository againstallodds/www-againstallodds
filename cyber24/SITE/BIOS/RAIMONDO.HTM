Lois Raimondo

VIETNAM

Lois Raimondo is working now as chief photographer for the Associated Press in Hanoi, Vietnam. She has published one book, The Little Lama of Tibet, and her work has appeared in Life, The New York Times, Der Spiegel and Paris-Match. In 1988, she was a Pulitzer Prize finalist for her work as an investigative reporter. Her backround also includes extensive Asian studies, comparitive literature, writing and history. She has spent time as a teacher, reporter, and a photographer and has received many awards for her work.
