Peter Charlesworth

THAILAND

Peter Charlesworth has been photographing Southeast Asia and the Pacific Rim for over a decade. Based in Bangkok covering many of the world's fastest growing economies, his ceaseless travels around the region finds his work published in such magazines as Time, Newsweek, and The New York Times.
