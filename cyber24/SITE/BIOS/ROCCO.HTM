Denise Rocco

UNITED STATES

A travel, documentary, and fine art photographer, Denise Rocco was most recently involved with the production of the book and interactive CD-ROM Passage to Vietnam. Her photographs from the project were published in several publications including American Photo, Popular Photography, Forbes, Step-by-Step Graphics, and Orient Express Magazine. Previously, Rocco received a grant from the Polaroid Corporation to photograph the public housing developments of Boston. Counter Culture, her documentary project about American diners, has appeared in numerous galleries across the United States.
