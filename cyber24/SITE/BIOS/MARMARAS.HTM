John Marmaras

AUSTRALIA

John Marmaras was involved in the original Day in the Life of Australia book and subsequently photographed Day in the Life book projects in America, Italy, and Ireland as well as The Power to Heal, Olympus One Moment and Apple - Ten Years (all projects with a similar international 24 hour approach). He has had extensive experience both in magazine and corporate photography, working for The London Sunday Times & Telegraph Magazines and especially Fortune Magazine throughout the years he lived in London and New York. John is now based in Sydney and last year covered assignments in twelve countries.
