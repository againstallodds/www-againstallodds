Daniel Meadows

GREAT BRITAIN

Daniel Meadows is probably best know for his traveling photo studio in a converted double-decker bus (1974-75). He is a photographer and a teacher of photographers. He worked throughout the 1980s with Magnum photographer David Hurn, teaching documentary photography at Newport's School of Art and Design. In 1994, he moved to Cardiff University and set up a new postgraduate program in photojournalism which he now teaches. Meadows has also had a successful career as a stillsman in the film industry. His photographs are collected in three books, the most recent of which, Set Pieces, deals with film stills (BFI publications 1993).
