Scott Thode

UNITED STATES

Scott Thode is a dedicated photographer whose photographs have appeared in The New York Times Sunday Magazine,Life, Newsweek, Stern, Il Venerdi and many other North American and European publications. His work has been exhibited at the Visa Pour L'image photo festival in Perpignon, France, in the Electric Blanket AIDS Project, the Colonnade Gallery in Washington D.C. and at P.S. 122 Gallery in New York City. Scott lives in New York City with his wife, Kathy Ryan, and their dog, Buster.
