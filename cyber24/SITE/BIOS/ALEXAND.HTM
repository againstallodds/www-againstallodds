Neil Alexander

UNITED STATES

Neil Alexander's career has included work as a merchant seaman and producing award winning documentaries for public television. Currently his photography business focuses on both editorial and corporate assignments. His photographs will be featured in two books in 1996. "Something Old, Something New" a profile of food and culture in Louisiana and "The University District", a new volume in a series about the architecture of New Orleans. His work has also appeared in Travel Holiday, Southern Accents, Historic Preservation and other regional and national magazines.
