Nick Kelsh

UNITED STATES

Nick Kelsh`s work has appeared in Time, Life, Newsweek, National Geographic, Stern, Der Spiegel, Paris Match, Forbes, Fortune and BusinessWeek magazines, and he has received numerous photographic awards. He has participated in several Day in the Life projects including China, Italy, Thailand, and Israel. He is the first photographer in the history of these projects to be honored with the covers of more than one book -- he has four. Nick holds a Bachelor of Journalism degree from the University of Missouri School of Journalism.
