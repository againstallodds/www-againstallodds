Michael Bryant

UNITED STATES

Michael Bryant was runner-up for Photographer of the Year in 1984 and 1988. He won the Photographer of the Year award in Michigan in 1980, California in 1983, and Pennsylvania in 1989. He shot for the Day in the Life book series on California, China, Italy, and Ireland, in which his work appeared on the cover.
