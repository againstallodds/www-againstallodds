David Falconer

UNITED STATES

David Falconer was a staff photographer for The Oregonian for 25 years. He is a freelancer with work published in the National Geographic, Sunset, People, Boys Life, and the NorthWest Airlines magazine. His work was included in the National Geographic Society 100 Year book. David was the second place recipient of the 1995 Journalism Pop Photography Award. He has also received numerous awards from the Society of American Travel Writers.
