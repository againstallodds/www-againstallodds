Joe Baraban

UNITED STATES

Joe Baraban is a freelance corporate advertising photographer based in Houston, Texas. For 25 years Joe's strong graphic style has sent him around the world photographing for such clients as: Dewars, Philip Morris, Toyota, Honda, Budweiser, Acura, Jaguar, Hennessy, Motorola, Coca Cola and United Airlines. He has also had stories published in National Geographic, Life, Geo, Time and Newsweek, and has been featured in Communication Arts, Portfolio and Photo Design. Joe brings to photography a fine-arts background, having studied painting and design at the Kansas City Art Institute. Even today Joe says,"I still consider myself a painter; I just use a camera instead of a paintbrush."
