Dustin Pittman

UNITED STATES

After studying film/video/photography at the School of Visual Arts in New York City, Dustin Pittman opened his New York studio. An Editorial and Advertising Photographer with a special interest in technique, he is known for incorporating an original sense of life, movement, and spontanious energy in all of his work. Dustin's photographs have appeared in Life, Vogue, Bazaar, W, New York Times Fashion Magazine, and many other publications. Dustin has a full service multi-media production studio in New York handing interactive as well as linear presentations.
