William Campbell

UNITED STATES

William Campbell is a photographer who covers social and environmental issues forTime as well as many other national and international publications. Formerly based in Africa, Campbell now lives in the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains. He is the recipient of the World Press and NPPA awards.
