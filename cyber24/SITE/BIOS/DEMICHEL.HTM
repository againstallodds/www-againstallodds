Guglielmo De' Micheli

ITALY

Guglielmo de'Micheli is a freelance photojournalist based in Italy. His work has appeared in several major U.S. and European publications, including Sports Illustrated, Time, Life, Geo, Capital, and many Day in the Life projects. He is a regular contributor to Forbes magazine. For his work in Day in the Life of Ireland, de'Micheli received an Award of Excellence from Communication Arts magazine.
