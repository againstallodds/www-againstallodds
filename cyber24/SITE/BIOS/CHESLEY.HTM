Paul Chesley

UNITED STATES

Paul Chesley is a freelance photographer based in Aspen, Colorado. He has worked with the National Geographic Society since 1975 and travels regularly to Europe and Asia. Solo exhibitions of his work have appeared in museums in London, Tokyo, and New York. Chesley has worked on 13 Day in the Life book projects. His work appears regularly in Time, Fortune, Life, Newsweek, and Geo magazines. He also photographed for the book project Passage to Vietnam which has an accompanying CD-ROM.
