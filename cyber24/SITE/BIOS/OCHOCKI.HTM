Gregory Ochocki

UNITED STATES

Greg Ochoki specializes in still marine photography. His work has taken him to Costa Rica, Micronesia, the Florida Keys, and the Amazon.

Ochoki's work has been published in Discover, Diving Magazine, Explorations, No Limits World Magazine, Sea World, Nature Conservancy Magazine, National Wildlife Magazine, and Tauchen Magazine, among others.
