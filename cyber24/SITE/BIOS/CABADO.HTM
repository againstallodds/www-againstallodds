Pablo Cabado


ARGENTINA

Pablo Cabado was born in Argentina. He has exhibited his work in Mexico, France, Argentina, the United States, England, Cuba, Italy, and Cyberspace. He has been awarded the Argentinean Antorchas Foundation Creative Artistic Grant, the Mother Jones International Documentary Photography Award, and the Young Photographer of the Year award from the International Center of Photography. Cabado's work has been published in Facts, Editorial La Azotea, Vis a Vis, Liberation, Metallarbetaren, Life, Between Worlds, and Mother Jones.
