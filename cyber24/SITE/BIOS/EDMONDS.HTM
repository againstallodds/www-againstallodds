Ron Edmonds

UNITED STATES

Ron Edmonds started his professional career as a staff photographer with the Honolulu Star Bulletin, where he worked from 1977-1980. He spent three years with U.P.I., and has been an Associated Press photographer since 1980. He won the 1982 Pulitzer Prize for Spot News, the National Headliners Award, and has been the recipient of the World Press, NPPA, and White House Photographers awards.
