William Mercer Mcleod

William Mercer Mcleod is primarily an editorial photographer, shooting for publications such as Time, Life, Rolling Stone, Wired, Premiere, and Entertainment Weekly. His favorite subject is his daughter Ava Ray. He lives in San Francisco with his girlfriend Kathy and their two daughters, Ava Ray and Ella (one month old), as well as Licorice, their cat.
