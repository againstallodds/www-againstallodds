David Gottschalk

Gottschalk graduated from the University of Nebraska at Omaha in 1985 with a Bachelor of Arts degree. Gottschalk worked for Plano Star Courier, McKinney Courier Gazette, the Texarkana Gazette and, in the fall of 1990, joined the Arkansas Democrat-Gazette.

Gottschalk has consistently won both in-state and regional awards. He specializes in daily spot news and feature work, but on occassion has excelled in sports photography.

His recent coverage of events include the Oklahoma City bombing, the 1994 & 1995 Final Four and the 1996 Super Bowl. Gottschalk has currently finished ninth in the National Press Photographers Region 7 Clip Contest leading the rest of the staff at the Arkansas Democrat-Gazette.
