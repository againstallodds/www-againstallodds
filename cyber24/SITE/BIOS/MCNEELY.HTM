Bob McNeely

UNITED STATES

Bob McNeely currently serves as the director of White House Photographic Services and personal photographer to President Bill Clinton. His photographic career began in the United States Army while he was an infantryman in Vietnam assigned to a ranger batallion. After his discharge from the army, he returned to the States to work as a photographer in New York and California. His work has appeared in national and international magazines, including the New York Times, Newsweek, Time, Vogue and Life magazine.
