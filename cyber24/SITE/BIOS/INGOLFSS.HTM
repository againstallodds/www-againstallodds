Einar Falur Ingolfsson


ICELAND

Einar Ingolfsson was born in Iceland in 1966. He received his MFA in photography from the School of Visual Arts in New York in 1994, and has been employed as Picture Editor for Morgunbladid, Iceland's biggest newspaper, since 1995. He has worked for Morgunbladid since 1992, part-time and full-time, as a photographer and writer on arts and literature. His photographs have been published in numerous magazines, books and newspapers, in Iceland and abroad. His work has exhibited in New York, Iceland, Sweden, and Germany.
