Look Inside the Book

Available exclusively at Amazon.com, THE OBAMA TIME CAPSULE offers a fascinating glimpse at the future of publishing.

There are many great large format photography books about President Obama's journey to the White House but what makes this one different from every other book is that THE OBAMA TIME CAPSULE enables you to weave your own name, your dedication, personal photographs and even your kid's artwork into Obama's inspiring story. Every single copy of this fascinating book is personalized for each book buyer, and each copy is printed one at a time.

The book features photographs of Obama's journey to the White House, shot my more than 140 0f America's leading photojournalists. Also included in the book are personal essays and commentary from Arianna Huffinton, Joe Klein, General Colin Powell, and even Obama's sister Auma.

Spanning a three year period from the tumultuous campaign, through Obama's first 100 days, this fascinating book captures the challenges and opportunities facing America's 44th President as he takes his place on the world stage.

The book offers readers seven different ways to personalize the book by adding their own content to what industry pundits expect to become an instant collectible. Unique customization features include:

A personalized dedication carries a message written by each book buyer

  • Each book buyer is listed on the cover and title page as "co-author"
  • A photograph of the customer appears on the back cover of the book
  • A blackberry screen contains a personalized message from President Obama thanking the book buyer for his or her support
  • The book buyer is named on a personalized inauguration invitation
  • A photograph of each book buyer appears alongside noted Obama supporters such as Scarlett Johanssen, George Clooney and Tiger Woods.
  • Book buyers can add artwork by their children to a page of Obama related artwork from children around the country

    • "From grass roots Facebook campaigns to viral YouTube videos, the Obama campaign took the presidential race to a new level in terms of personalization," said Smolan, co-founder of Against All Odds Productions (AAO). "This book enables millions of people to seamlessly merge this moment in history with their own personal experiences."

    The OBAMA TIME CAPSULE (retail price $64.95) is available exclusively through Amazon at an introductory price of $34.95. For additional information: www.TheObamaTimeCapsule.com
